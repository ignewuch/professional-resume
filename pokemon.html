pokémon gallery

1. pikachu

pikachu is an electric-type pokémon. pikachu is known for its cute appearance and its ability to generate electricity. electric-type pokémon are very effective against flying and water-type pokémon and weak against ground-type pokémon.

2. charmander

charmander is a fire-type pokémon. charmander is recognized for its flame-tipped tail and its fiery spirit. fire-type pokémon are very effective against grass, bug, ice, and steel-type pokémon and are weaker against water, rock, and ground-type pokémon.

3. bulbasaur

bulbasaur is a grass and poison-type pokémon and is the first pokémon in the pokédex. bulbasaur is known for its teal color and spouting plant on its back. grass-type pokémon are effective against water, ground, and rock-type pokémon while being weaker to fire, bug, flying, and poison-type pokémon.

4. squirtle

squirtle is a water-type pokémon. squirtle is known for its playful nature and ability to shoot water from its mouth. water-type pokémon are super effective against fire, ground, and rock-type pokémon while being weaker to electric and grass-type pokémon.

5. jigglypuff

jigglypuff is a normal and fairy-type pokémon. jigglypuff is known for its ability to sing lullabies that make others fall asleep. since jigglypuff is both a normal and fairy-type pokémon, jigglypuff is super effective against fighting, dragon, and dark-type pokémon while weaker towards steel, poison, and fighting-type pokémon.
